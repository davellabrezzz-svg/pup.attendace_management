Transparency Seal

Posted in compliance with the annual Transparency Seal provision of the General Appropriations Act, which requires government agencies to publish key information on their mandate, officials, budget, and programs on their official website.

📂 Required Disclosures

I. Mandate, Officials & Contact Information

The branch's mandate and functions, and a directory of officials with their position, designation, and contact details.

Available — see Contact Us

II. Annual Reports

Yearly summary of the branch's accomplishments, enrollment, and operations for the past three years.

To be uploaded by Admin Office

III. Approved Budget & Targets

The branch's approved budget allocation and the corresponding performance targets for the year.

To be uploaded by Admin Office

IV. Major Programs & Projects

Programs and projects implemented by the branch, including their intended beneficiaries.

To be uploaded by Admin Office

V. Status of Implementation

Progress and evaluation reports for ongoing programs and projects.

To be uploaded by Admin Office

VI. Annual Procurement Plan & Awarded Contracts

List of planned procurements and contracts awarded to suppliers or contractors.

To be uploaded by Admin Office

VII. Budget & Financial Accountability Reports

Periodic financial reports on the utilization of the branch's allocated funds.

To be uploaded by Admin Office

VIII. Freedom of Information (FOI) Summary

Summary of FOI requests received by the campus and how they were acted upon.

To be uploaded by Admin Office

Documents marked "To be uploaded by Admin Office" are maintained by the campus Administrative Office and will be linked here once digitized. For copies in the meantime, please reach out through the Contact Us page.
