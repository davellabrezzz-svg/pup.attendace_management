Good Governance

PUP Maragondon Branch — accountability, transparency, and public service

As a unit of a state university, the PUP Maragondon Branch upholds the principles of good governance set out in Republic Act No. 6713 (Code of Conduct and Ethical Standards for Public Officials and Employees), Republic Act No. 9485 (Anti-Red Tape Act of 2007) as amended by Republic Act No. 11032 (Ease of Doing Business and Efficient Government Service Delivery Act of 2018), and the yearly Transparency Seal provision of the General Appropriations Act. These laws guide how the campus delivers services, handles public funds, and reports its activities to students, faculty, and the community it serves.

🎯 Vision & Mission

Vision

To be recognized as a leading center of inclusive, quality, and innovative higher education that develops globally competitive graduates while remaining rooted in service to the Filipino community.

Mission

To provide equitable access to higher education and to advance instruction, research, and extension programs that respond to the needs of students and the local community of Maragondon and the wider province of Cavite.

📜 Citizen's Charter

The Citizen's Charter is an official document that lists each frontline service offered by the campus — such as registration, records requests, and enrollment assistance — together with the requirements, processing time, fees (if any), and the office responsible.
